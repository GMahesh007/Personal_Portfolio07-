Digital Devotee

  • Tech Stack: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, MySQL
  • GitHub URL: Project Link

Aim

The primary objective of "Digital Devotee" is to bridge the gap between traditional temple practices and modern technology. The platform enhances the temple experience for devotees by integrating various modules for efficient temple management. The goal is to offer a hassle-free, modernized solution while upholding the sanctity of religious rituals and services.

Modules

  1. Seva Module: Allows devotees to book and manage seva services (prayers, rituals, ceremonies) with an online payment system and personalized scheduling options.
  2. Hundhi Management: Provides temple administrators with a system for transparent management of donations and contributions, ensuring accountability.
  3. Rooms Management: Facilitates accommodation booking and management for devotees during their religious visits.
  4. Volunteers Module: Organizes and coordinates volunteers for temple activities, with registration and approval by administrators.
  5. Donations Management: Simplifies donation tracking and offers multiple payment options. Administrators can manage and monitor donation trends.

Conclusion

"Digital Devotee" streamlines temple operations and enhances the experience for both devotees and administrators. By integrating traditional services with modern technology, it fosters a more efficient and eco-friendly approach to managing temple activities.
